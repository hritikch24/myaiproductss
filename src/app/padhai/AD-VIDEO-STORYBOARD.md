# Padhai Ad Video Storyboard

Two versions: one for students, one for parents. 30-45 seconds each. Works for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and WhatsApp Status.

---

## VIDEO 1: Student Version (30s)
**Hook: First 3 seconds are everything**

### Scene 1 (0-3s) — THE HOOK
- **Visual**: Close-up of phone showing a WhatsApp message from "Papa" — "Beta, padhai ho rahi hai?"
- **Text overlay**: Bold text "SOUNDS FAMILIAR?"
- **Audio**: Notification sound → dramatic pause

### Scene 2 (3-7s) — THE PROBLEM
- **Visual**: Student sitting at desk, books open, looking confused/overwhelmed. Camera pans across scattered notes, multiple textbooks.
- **Voiceover (Hindi)**: "Coaching laga di, books kharid li... par kitna syllabus baaki hai, pata hai?"
- **Text overlay**: "40% students don't finish their JEE/NEET syllabus"

### Scene 3 (7-12s) — THE REVEAL
- **Visual**: Student opens Padhai on phone. Screen recording shows the syllabus page — all subjects, chapters listed, progress bar.
- **Voiceover**: "Ab pata chalega — chapter by chapter, subject by subject"
- **Text overlay**: "See EXACTLY what's done and what's pending"

### Scene 4 (12-18s) — THE FEATURES (Quick cuts)
- **Cut 1**: Tapping a chapter as "done" → green checkmark animation (2s)
- **Cut 2**: Taking a quick quiz → "82% — Well studied!" result (2s)
- **Cut 3**: Dashboard showing streak "12 days" with fire animation (2s)
- **Voiceover**: "Mark chapters done, prove it with quizzes, build your streak"

### Scene 5 (18-24s) — THE PARENT ANGLE
- **Visual**: Split screen — student studying on left, parent's phone on right showing Sunday report.
- **Voiceover**: "Aur Sunday ko parents ko report automatically chala jaata hai"
- **Text overlay**: "No nagging. Just facts."

### Scene 6 (24-30s) — THE CTA
- **Visual**: Padhai logo animation. Phone showing the "Start Tracking Free" button.
- **Voiceover**: "100+ students already tracking. Tu kab shuru kar raha hai?"
- **Text overlay**: "kraftai.in/padhai — FREE. 30 seconds to start."
- **Audio**: Upbeat end sound

## VIDEO 2: Parent Version (30s)
**Target: WhatsApp groups of parents, Facebook, Instagram**

### Scene 1 (0-3s) — THE HOOK
- **Visual**: Parent looking worried, sitting with chai, phone in hand.
- **Text overlay (Hindi)**: "Coaching ki fees: ₹2,50,000. Result: ???"
- **Audio**: Tension music

### Scene 2 (3-8s) — THE PROBLEM
- **Visual**: Quick montage —
  - Parent asking "Padhai ki?" → Child saying "Ho gayi" (annoyed face)
  - Parent looking at coaching bill
  - Parent asking other parents "Tumhara beta kaha tak pahucha?"
- **Voiceover (Hindi)**: "Roz puchte ho 'kya padha'. Roz sunta hai 'sab ho gaya'. Par sach kya hai?"

### Scene 3 (8-14s) — THE SOLUTION
- **Visual**: Parent opens kraftai.in/padhai/track. Types invite code. Dashboard loads showing child's progress.
- **Voiceover**: "Ab apne phone pe dekho — kitne chapters hue, quiz mein kitne marks aaye, kitne din streak hai"
- **Text overlay**: "Real progress, not just promises"

### Scene 4 (14-20s) — THE REPORT
- **Visual**: Sunday 7PM notification arrives. Parent opens it — beautiful weekly report showing: 5 chapters done, 82% quiz score, 7-day streak.
- **Voiceover**: "Har Sunday 7 baje ek report aata hai — shanti se, bina jhagda kiye"
- **Text overlay**: "Weekly report — automatic"

### Scene 5 (20-26s) — THE EMOTIONAL HIT
- **Visual**: Parent smiling looking at phone. Then shows the phone to their spouse. "Dekho, is hafte 5 chapters kiye"
- **Voiceover**: "Ab pata hai ki paisa kaha ja raha hai. Aur bacche ko bhi pata hai ki koi dekh raha hai."
- **Text overlay**: "Accountability without pressure"

### Scene 6 (26-30s) — THE CTA
- **Visual**: Padhai logo. "Ask your child to sign up. Share their invite code with you."
- **Text overlay**: "kraftai.in/padhai — 100% FREE"
- **Voiceover**: "Abhi apne bacche ko bhejo. Free hai, 30 second mein shuru."
